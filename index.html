ABOUT MY LIFE

A Paragraph Spoken by the Man Himself

Hi! My name is Metroboy and my job is to keep the city of Metropolis safe from the crime whose goal it is to destroy it. Ah yes, there have been villians that have tried ruining the city ever since it was first founded way back in 1948. My dad Metroman had the job as superhero of the city, but he eventually retired and same the position to me as his son. The citizens of Metropolis are in my hands, and I must protect them just as my father did!


ABOUT METROPOLIS

Happy 70th Anniversary!

For seventy long years, the city has been in good hands between either me or my father. We have fought off crime ranging in all forms, shapes and sizes, big to small. And through crime-fighting, we have both learned that the most valuable thing to have is courage to our jobs, as the citizens of the city have to us. Have the courage to fight on!
